Blueberry Compote

Ingredients

  • 5 pints blueberries
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 5 tsp lemon juice
  • 10 Tbsp water
  • 2½ tsp vanilla
  • 5 tsp ginger
  • 2 tsp cinnamon

Method

  1. Add all ingredients to a pot.
  2. Simmer until the blueberries break down and the mixture thickens to desired consistency.
No. 02

Teriyaki Sauce

Large Batch

Ingredients

  • 6 cups soy sauce
  • 32 oz brown sugar (~4.25 cups packed)
  • 22–24 cloves garlic (~8 Tbsp minced)
  • 7 Tbsp fresh grated ginger
  • 12 cups water
  • 1⅓ cups cornstarch

Method

  1. Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and water in a large pot.
  2. Bring to a gentle simmer.
  3. Mix cornstarch with a little cold water to make a slurry.
  4. Slowly whisk slurry into simmering sauce until thickened.
  5. Cool before storing.
No. 03

Classic Tuna Salad

Yield: ~10–12 quarts · 50–65 sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 8 lb 5 oz (133 oz) tuna
  • 8 cups mayo (start with 7, adjust)
  • 4 cups diced celery
  • 2 cups diced red onion
  • ½ cup sweet relish (optional)
  • 3–4 Tbsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp black pepper
  • ½ cup lemon juice

Method

  1. Drain tuna very well.
  2. Combine tuna, celery, onion, and relish (if using).
  3. Fold in mayo gradually, starting with 7 cups.
  4. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Mix thoroughly and adjust seasoning.
  6. Chill at least 2 hours before serving.

Aioli

Large Batch — Scalable Base

Ingredients

  • 6 cups mayo

  • ½ cup lemon juice
  • 6 tsp salt
  • 4 tsp black pepper

Method

  1. Mince garlic very fine, or paste it for a smoother texture.

  4. Chill at least 1–2 hours before serving to let flavors develop.

No. 06

Roasted Butternut Squash

Italian-inspired seasoning · Serves 25

Ingredients

  • 10 lbs butternut squash, diced
  • 1 cup (240ml) olive oil
  • 2½ Tbsp salt
  • 1½ Tbsp black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp dried rosemary
  • 1 tsp nutmeg

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss diced squash with olive oil until evenly coated.
  3. Add salt, pepper, rosemary, and nutmeg. Toss until well distributed.
  4. Spread in a single layer across 4–5 sheet pans. Do not crowd.
  5. Roast 25–30 minutes, flipping halfway, until edges are golden and caramelized.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning before serving.
Notes: Fresh rosemary: double to ~4 Tbsp if substituting fresh for dried. Nutmeg is intentionally light — add more after tasting the first pan. Toss in batches if your bowl isn't large enough.
No. 07

Succotash

Ingredients

  • 4 pints cherry tomatoes
  • 40 oz corn kernels
  • 40 oz baby lima beans
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 2–2½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 1½ tsp garlic powder

Method

  1. Melt butter in a large pot or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic powder and smoked paprika, stirring 30 seconds to bloom the spices.
  3. Add lima beans and corn, cooking 5–7 minutes until heated through.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes and cook 3–5 minutes until they soften and burst.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Taste and adjust before serving.
